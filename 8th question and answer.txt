8.  How could you display only the records of service rep 39 in the Client Financial Form?

ANSWER : 

OPEN THE CLIENT FINANCIAL FORM AND GO TO SERVICE REP NUMBER RECORD . 
TAP RIGHT CLICK ON THAT FIELD . SELECT THE OPTION TEXT FILTERS AND CLICK ON 
EQUALS . A BOX NAMED CUSTOM FILTER WILL APPEAR , THEN TYPE THE RESPECTED
SERVICE REP NUMBER  39 .
