
8. The Service Rep table includes a Start Date field in the format, mm/dd/yyyy. How could 
you add an input mask for the Start Date field?

ANSWER : 
For adding an input mask to start date field . you need to open the service rep table in 
design view and under the general , you can see the input mask option . click on the 3dots which 
is in end of the row and a box will appear , select the option " short date " on it .
